Three new co-marketing ideas for our clients:

  • CROSS-COMPANY TRAINING PROGRAMS: joint training sessions or workshops for the employees of Halborn and partner companies. Imagine how cool a Hackerhouse could be if it included cross-company sessions while sharing space and activities throughout a full weekend!
  • HALBORN TALKS (PODCAST) although Halborn already has similar approaches as part of its social media strategy (with content like the CI/CD Series and its Flash Videos), an out-of-our-niche podcast could come in handy. This strategy has shown success in the past when implemented by other brands (Nude Project is a great example). Branching out from our niche could boost our brand awareness.
  • INFLUENCER MARKETING: what has worked in the past and for other brands will surely continue to work and prove to be a winning strategy. This is the case with influencer marketing. In this context, Halborn's co-marketing strategy would not be aimed precisely at collaborations or partnerships with our clients, but with social media influencers who are (even if vaguely) related to the sector and can add a touch of freshness and joviality to our brand.
